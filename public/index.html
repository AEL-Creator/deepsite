About Brooklyn's Bimmers

We are a local NYC-based Bimmer programming service specializing in modern vehicle electronics and keys. Our mission is to provide dealership-quality service at a fraction of the price, with the convenience of mobile service throughout the New York City area.

With years of experience working exclusively with Bimmers, we understand the unique challenges these vehicles present when it comes to keys and electronic modules. Our technicians are equipped with the latest tools and software to handle even the most complex programming tasks.

We pride ourselves on fast, honest, and affordable solutions. Whether you need a simple key duplicate or complex module programming, we've got you covered with the same precision as the dealership without the dealership prices.
