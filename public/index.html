Frequently Asked Questions

What if I lost all my BMW keys?

We can make and program a new key even if no working key exists. Our specialized equipment allows us to recover your vehicle's security data and create new keys on the spot.

Do you come to me?

Yes, we're a fully mobile service covering all 5 boroughs of NYC - Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, Bronx, and Staten Island. We come to your home, office, or wherever your vehicle is located.

How long does it take?

Most key replacement jobs take under 45 minutes. Module programming may take up to 1 hour depending on the vehicle. We work efficiently to minimize your downtime.

Do you do other brands?

Currently, we specialize only in BMW vehicles. This specialization allows us to provide the highest quality service with the most up-to-date equipment for BMW models.

How much does a new key cost?

Prices vary depending on your BMW model and key type, but our prices are typically 40-60% less than dealership prices. Contact us for a specific quote for your vehicle.
